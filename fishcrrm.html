COMPONENT 1: Fisheries and Coastal Resilient Resource Planning and Management

Sub-component 1.1: Ecosystem Approach to Fisheries Management Planning and Institutions

1. Fisheries Resource Management Planning Workshop for Sardine Stock in FMA 6

To facilitate the development of the harvest strategy concept in fisheries management, a workshop was conducted on January 21-23, 2025, by the Fisheries Management Research and Development Division (FMRED) and the National Stock Assessment Program (NSAP) to draft the Fisheries Management Plan (FMP) for sardines. This collaborative effort brought together stakeholders, scientists, and policymakers to discuss data, share insights, and formulate effective management guidelines tailored to these critical species. The workshop was attended by representatives from IFARMC Cavite and Batangas, fisherfolk representatives, BFAR (CO, NCR, Region 3, Region IV-A), NFRDI-NSAP, the Science Advisory Group (SAG), and FishRight.

2. Stakeholder Consultation on the Proposed Harvest Strategy for the Management and Conservation of Mackerel Scad

A stakeholder consultation workshop was held in Nasugbu, Batangas, on March 10-12, 2025, to provide updated data on the status of mackerel scad in FMA 6, present management options, and gather stakeholder feedback to refine the Fisheries Management Plan (FMP). Participants included commercial and municipal fisherfolk leaders from Nasugbu and Lian, representatives from the LGUs of Lian and Nasugbu, as well as FCU4A, FCU3, RPIU6, and FMA 6 SAG.

Sub-component 1.2: Aquaculture Development and Management

1. Aquafarm Profiling

The Aquafarm Profiling activity in FCU4A is complete, and the enumerator is now encoding the data into the Aquaculture Fisheries Geospatial Information System (AqFGIS).
